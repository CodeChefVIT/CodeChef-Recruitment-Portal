CodeChef is a not-for-profit educational initiative by Directi, an Indian software company. In Vellore Institute of Technology, CodeChef is a technical chapter that helps students all over the campus to improve their coding skills. The core committee members of the chapters are divided into four areas of expertise- Technical, Projects, Management and Design. The main objective is to provide the students a platform where they can learn, share and test their knowledge. CodeChefVIT keeps conducting several workshops and events in order to help the students do the same.
